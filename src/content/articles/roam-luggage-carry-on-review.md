# Roam Luggage Carry-On Review: The Customizable Option

*[Estimated read time: 8 minutes]*

**Quick Answer**: Roam Luggage offers the most customizable carry-ons on the market, allowing you to choose colors, materials, and features. At $295-$395, they compete with Away and Monos. The quality is excellent, but customization adds wait time.

## What Makes Roam Different

Roam Luggage took the luggage industry by storm with their fully customizable approach. Instead of choosing from preset colors, you can design your perfect bag:

- **Shell colors**: 15+ options
- **Trim colors**: Match or contrast
- **Handle colors**: Multiple options
- **Wheel colors**: Several choices
- **Interior colors**: Multiple fabrics

This level of personalization is unique in the luggage market.

## Our Testing Results

We tested the Roam Classic for 6 months with frequent business travel. Here is what we found:

### Build Quality: 8.5/10

The polycarbonate shell is solid and protective. The Hinomoto wheels are smooth and quiet—among the best we have tested. Zippers are YKK quality and have held up well.

The customization does not compromise quality. Every component feels premium.

### Organization: 8/10

The interior is well-designed with:
- Compression system
- Mesh divider
- Accessory pockets
- Suiter compartment (optional)

The Flex model adds a useful front pocket that many competitors lack.

### Aesthetics: 9.5/10

This is where Roam shines. Being able to match your bag to your personal style is incredibly satisfying. The color options are beautiful, and the quality of the finish is excellent.

We designed ours with a matte black shell and rose gold trim—stunning.

### Durability: 8/10

After 6 months of regular use:
- No visible scratches on matte finish
- Wheels still smooth
- Zippers functioning perfectly
- No cosmetic issues

The 10-year warranty provides peace of mind.

### Travel Performance: 9/10

- Carries on easily (meets all airline dimensions)
- Wheels handle all floor types
- Handles are comfortable
- Light enough to lift overhead

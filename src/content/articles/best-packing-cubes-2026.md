## Why Packing Cubes Are Essential

If you have ever opened your suitcase to find your carefully folded shirts have become a wrinkled mess, packing cubes are about to change your life.

For business travelers, packing cubes offer:

- **Organization** — Separate clothes by type, day, or occasion
- **Compression** — Maximize carry-on space
- **Protection** — Keep clothes from shifting during travel
- **Efficiency** — Find what you need without unpacking everything

We tested 15+ packing cube sets over 6 months to find the best options for business travel.

## What to Look For

### Key Features

- **Compression zipper** — Reduces volume by up to 60%

## How to Use Packing Cubes for Business Travel

### The Business Traveler System

**Cube 1: Shirts**
- Roll each dress shirt tightly
- Use medium cube
- Place in carry-on opposite wheels

**Cube 2: Pants**
- Fold pants at crease
- Use large cube
- Place at bottom of bag

**Cube 3: Accessories**
- Underwear, socks, ties
- Use small cube
- Keep in front pocket

**Cube 4: Toiletries/tech**
- Keep separate from clothes
- Use small cube
- Easy access during security

### Pro Tips

1. **Color-code by trip type**: Blue for conferences, green for client visits
2. **Use compression fully**: Saves 30-60% volume
3. **Keep cube shape uniform**: Easier to fit in carry-on
4. **Wash between trips**: Maintains freshness

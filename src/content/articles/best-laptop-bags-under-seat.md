## Why Under-Seat Laptop Bags Matter

Here is the reality of business travel: overhead bin space is increasingly limited, boarding is chaotic, and the last thing you want is to be that person holding up the line because your bag did not fit.

A quality under-seat laptop bag solves these problems:

- **Guaranteed boarding** — No fighting for overhead space
- **Quick access** — Keep your laptop, documents, and essentials within reach
- **TSA-friendly** — Pull out your laptop at security without unpacking your entire bag
- **Versatile** — Works as a personal item on any airline

We tested dozens of bags to find the best options for every budget and use case.

## What to Look For

### Dimensions That Fit

Most airlines allow personal items up to:
- **18" x 14" x 8"** (standard)
- Always check your specific airline

The key is finding a bag that fits under the seat in front of you while still holding your essentials.

### Essential Features

- Padded laptop compartment (fits your specific laptop size)
- Easy-access front pocket for documents
- Comfortable carrying options (crossbody, shoulder, hand)
- Durability for daily use
- Professional appearance

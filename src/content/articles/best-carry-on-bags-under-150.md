## What to Look for in a Budget Business Carry-On

### Professional Appearance

The bag shouldn't look budget. Look for matte finishes in neutral colors—navy, black, charcoal. Avoid excessive branding or glossy plastics that scream "discount." A quality bag should look like it cost more than it did.

### Durability That Survives Real Travel

Business travel means your bag gets handled by airport staff, tossed into overhead bins, and rolled across countless terminal floors. Priority features:

- **Quality zippers**: YKK brand zippers are industry standard for durability
- **Reinforced corners**: Where bags fail first
- **Solid wheel assemblies**: Four spinner wheels should feel smooth, not wobbly
- **Warranty coverage**: A manufacturer's willingness to stand behind their product speaks volumes

### Carry-On Compliant Dimensions

Most U.S. airlines allow carry-ons up to 22" x 14" x 9" (including wheels and handles). International carriers vary—European airlines often allow slightly smaller bags. Always verify your specific airline before buying.

### Practical Features for Business Travel

- **Suiter compartment**: Keeps suits and dresses wrinkle-free
- **Laptop compartment**: Quick access through TSA without fully opening your bag
- **Front pocket**: For documents, laptop, and items you need during boarding
- **Expandability**: A few extra inches can save you on checked bag fees

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I use a carry-on under $150 for international business travel?

Yes. All bags reviewed meet standard airline carry-on dimensions. However, some international carriers have tighter restrictions—always check before flying. Budget airlines in Europe often have stricter size limits.

### Do these bags have warranty coverage?

All options include manufacturer warranties ranging from 1 year to lifetime coverage. We recommend registering your purchase to ensure warranty coverage.

### What's the best color for business travel?

Navy, black, and charcoal gray are most professional. Avoid bright colors unless you're in a creative industry where personal expression is valued.

### Should I choose hard shell or soft shell?

Hard shell offers better protection for electronics and fragile items. Soft shell typically provides more flexibility, better organization, and the ability to fit into tighter spaces. For business travel, we slightly prefer soft shell for the organization features.

### How often should I replace my carry-on?

With proper care, a quality carry-on should last 5-7 years of frequent business travel. Signs you need replacement include broken wheels, damaged zippers, torn fabric, or compromised handles.
